Client

How did you decide what content and images to use for your site plan?

I searched other sites that went through solving a Rubik's cube and found the images that I thought would be helpful for others to learn it more effectively.

How did you communicate with your developer? Was there any issues with communication?

We used email and it was perfectly effective, I didn't see any problems. He was very good at responding to requests.

Did your developer meet your expectations for your site?

Yes, my site looks very close to the wire frame and it works well.

What will you do differently next time you hire a team to do a project for you?

I will make sure to just have more direct communitcation with the developers and ensure that they're in more contact with each other.

Lead Developer

How did you manage your development team and comunicate the tasks for your junior devs?

I used email, and sent them the site plan highlighted so they knew what they needed to do for their sections.

How did you communicate with your client? Was there any issues?

I used email, and she was able to view the site at all times so she could correct anything she saw.

How did you handle publishing your clients site and sharing it with the client?

I created a github repository and shared the url with the client.

How was your experience with managing your site with GitHub?

It was very easy to use and update the site files.

Junior Developer

Were you able to contribute to your sites?

Did you run into any issues with GitHub and adding your changes to the sites?

General Experience and Reflections

What was the most challenging part of this project for you? Were you able to overcome it?

The most challenging part was making the site plan since I'm not any sort of designer but in the end it turned out really good.

What did you enjoy most about this project?

I really liked taking the info from a site plan and making a page out of it.

What would you do differently if you could do the project again?

I would make sure to get the info from the client earlier on.

Do you feel more confident in working on real-world projects after this experience? Explain.

Yes. I know how important the site plan is and will make sure any client gets one to me as early in the design as possible.
